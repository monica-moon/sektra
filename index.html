The House of Sektra

Perfume, in Animal Form

Sektra was founded in Paris on a single idea: that a scent should arrive with a face. Each composition is poured into a flacon shaped like the creature that inspired it, cast in glass and brass and finished entirely by hand.

The maison works in small batches, resting every extrait eleven months before it is numbered and released. No two flacons are exactly alike, and only five hundred of each will ever exist.

Three creatures. Six compositions. One belief, worn on the skin: that the wild was never meant to be tamed, only bottled.
